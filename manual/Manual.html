Time Freezer Manual

What it is

Time Freezer is a realtime audio program to freeze (hold) notes.

Developed by Triceratupuz Lab

Key features

  • Wxpython interface and cSound audio engine
  • Inspired by EHX Freeze - Sound Retainer
  • 8 note freezers
